About

I am a Postdoctoral Associate in Mathematics in Copos Lab at Northeastern University. I am interested in modeling, numerical analysis, and simulation of microscopic cellular processes, immune receptor signaling pathways, cell locomation, and applications of immersed boundary methods in red blood cell motion. My research interests include mathematical biology, PDEs/ODEs, stochastic processes, and numerical solutions to partial differential equations
