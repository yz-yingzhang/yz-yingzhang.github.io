My research interests are in the areas of differential equations (ordinary and partial), stochastic processes, numerical analysis, and mathematical modeling in biology. I am especially interested in building mathematical models to study macroscopic biological systems and developing accurate and efficient numerical approximations to the solutions of partial and ordinary differential equations for micro-scale stochastic systems. Recently my research has focused on the development of mechanical models to study cooperative cell migration, applications of particle-based stochastic models to transport processes on dynamic biological networks, as well as the development of computational models to study the coagulation system under different pathological scenarios.
